In Spring 2027, the Gaynor Lab will get started at Florida State University in the Department of Biological Science. My lab is open to both PhD and master students through the Ecology and Evolution area in Biological Sciences at FSU. I also have funding to support a postdoctoral researcher and a technician during my first three years at FSU. If you are interested in joining the lab, please send me an email with a CV and a brief explanation of your research interests and how these align with the lab.
